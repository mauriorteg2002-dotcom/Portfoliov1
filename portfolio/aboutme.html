About Me

I'm Mauricio Ortega, a Mexican-American designer based in Utah with a passion for creating art that's accessible to everyone. I love designing for the everyday person and believe that good design should be both meaningful and enjoyable. I value collaboration and believe the best work comes from sharing ideas and working together. As a designer, I'm always looking to grow, refine my skills, and continue learning so I can create work that elevates both brands and communities.
